# 3-Month Roadmap: Scene-to-Brand

The goal is to deliver a polished tool and drive adoption through design communities, targeting $500-$1000 in 'coffee' donations.

## Month 1: Foundation & Core Experience
*Goal: MVP release & community validation.*
- **Week 1-2:** Build core image upload, processing, and palette display.
- **Week 3:** Implement clipboard copy and basic export (JSON).
- **Week 4:** Initial launch on Product Hunt/Reddit (r/webdev, r/design).
- **KPI:** 100+ active users.

## Month 2: Feature Polish & Viral Hooks
*Goal: User engagement & retention.*
- **Week 5-6:** Add Tailwind config generator & CSS variable export (direct copy-paste utility).
- **Week 7:** Add a "share palette" feature (generated permalink images).
- **Week 8:** Integration with design libraries (Figma/Sketch).
- **KPI:** 500+ daily active users, 10% returning users.

## Month 3: Growth & Monetization (Coffee)
*Goal: Community loyalty & donation conversion.*
- **Week 9:** Build a "Pro Tips" design blog inside the app to provide value beyond extraction.
- **Week 10:** Tighten UI/UX with professional design polish.
- **Week 11:** Direct Buy Me a Coffee / Ko-fi integration with a "Help me keep this ad-free" prompt.
- **Week 12:** Outreach to design influencers/tech YouTubers for features.
- **KPI:** Reach $500-$1000 in donations.
